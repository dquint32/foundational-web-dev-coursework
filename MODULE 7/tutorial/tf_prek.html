Pre-K Classes

As preschoolers gain more self-esteem, they feel ready to take on the world. Our preschool curriculum enhances their confidence by providing activities to help them become problem solvers and nurture a love of learning.

We're proud of the work we do. Early learning standards, backed by education experts, inform the scope and sequence of our pre-k program. Our curriculum aligns to 72 learning standards progressing sequentially across six developmental domains.

Language Skills

Language, literacy, and communication skills are embedded into a child's daily experiences. We strive to create meaningful classroom experiences that help children use and build a growing vocabulary.

Math Exploration

Pre-k children are enthusiastic mathematicians. Our instructors work to ensure that our students build mathematical understanding related to real, everyday problems.

Science Studies

Children develop and test their own theories and engage in scientific reasoning. Our curriculum encourages exploration across physics, chemistry, and earth science.

Creative Expressions

Pre-kindergarten is an ideal time to introduce artistic expression. We teach sculpting, book illustration, and dramatic storytelling with costumes and props.

Cultural Sharing

We foster emotional intelligence, compassion, and respect for diverse cultures, traditions, and lifestyles through inclusive classroom experiences.

Physical Wellness

Children learn about physical wellness through activity, healthy eating, and hygiene. Our curriculum encourages responsibility and self-awareness.
